EMBAKASICONSTITUENCY


Embakasi East Constituency lies south-east of Nairobi Province
and hosts Jomo Kenyatta International Airport (JKIA) which
is the biggest airport in east and central Africa and is the focal point
for major aviation activity in the region among other notable organizations.
The elected member of parliament is Hon. Babu Owino Paul Ongili.
